LUFTHANSA

Deutsche Lufthansa AG or Lufthansa is the flag carrier of Germany. It is the largest airline
in Europe in terms of overall passengers carried and fleet size. Apart from 18 domestic destinations,
this airline is operating services in 197 international destinations of 78 countries.
Lufthansa offers three classes of services on its flights, i.e., First Class, Business Class, Economy Class.
On the A-380 Airbus, the passengers of Economy Class can access internet service for a fee through Deutsche Telekom.
Indian Destinations Covered by Lufthansa: Bangalore, Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai and Pune
Checked Baggage Allowances in Lufthansa:
1 bag up to 23 kg for Economy Class

2 bags up to 32 kg each for Business Class
3 bags up to 32 kg each for First Class
Helpline: 1800 102 5838 (toll free) or +91 124 4888888 / +91 124 4888999
Website: www.lufthansa.com

Senator Lounge and Business Lounge. The sophisticated Swiss customary and values are reflected in “personal service”,
“in-depth quality” and “typical Swiss hospitality” of the airline.
